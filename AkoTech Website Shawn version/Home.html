Welcome to the AkoTech Website!

We are a subsidiary of Rolleston College, a group of students who strive to make technology easier to understand for the population of Rolleston and the wider community by collecting information about their problems with tech and submitting it to the company who owns the tech product, and propose some solutions to make the product better and more understandable.
